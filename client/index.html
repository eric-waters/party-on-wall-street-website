The Idea

Party on Wall Street is a couch-party style game where a single host who owns the game can display it on a larger screen for other players to view. These other players can then connecting using their own computer or mobile device in order to interact with the game. During the game, the players will:

  1. Invent solutions to randomly-generated problems
  2. Choose an invention created by another player
  3. Craft an enticing and persuasive pitch for their selected product
  4. See their product pitches face-off against other players and vote on their favorite
  5. Invest in products that have the most potential
  6. Reap rewards in ROI from smart investments

Implementation

Development is underway! The game is being created using the Unity game engine. We are also utilizing AirConsole in order to use phones as controllers. We have also hired a graphic designer to add art and characters to the game's interface. The implementation is indeed challenging and time consuming, but the game will be complete by April 15, 2022.

Business Strategy

Using AirConsole technologies to build this game means that we are required to post our game on AirConsole's game store. Although this is not ideal for our own profit, it is the only way the game will be completed in time. After April 15, we intend to remove the AirConsole technology in order to sell the game on its own in popular game stores like Playstation, Xbox, Steam, and more. As a brand new game studio, effective advertising will be essential to bringing this game to life. Our marketing strategy is still to be determined, but we anticipate that digital marketing, especially social media marketing, will be the most cost-effective way to reach our target market.
