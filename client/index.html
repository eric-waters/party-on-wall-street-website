Our Mission

To provide a refreshing and exciting new experience for the tycoons in society who want a little more of that entrepreneurial lifestyle.

Who are we?

We are a team of technically gifted ASU students living an entrepreneurial lifestyle and searching for a way to share that with other students like ourselves. We are avid supporters of personally directed business ventures and self-led projects, and as creators of our own destinies, we wanted to share that experience with others who have a passion for the self-made mindset. Our brand's goal is to provide a fun and relaxing experience that brings together individuals who share that drive, and to challenge each one of them and their ability to come up with unique and creative solutions on the fly. Our team is focused on providing an experience that resembles the entrepreneurial process while also participating in that same very process: a game for the players, made by the players.

The Idea

Party on Wall Street is a couch-party style game where a single host who owns the game can display it on a larger screen for other players to view. These other players can then connect using their own computer or mobile device in order to interact with the game. During the game, the players will:

  1. Invent solutions to randomly-generated problems
  2. Choose an invention created by another player
  3. Craft an enticing and persuasive pitch for their selected product
  4. See their product pitches face-off against other players and vote on their favorite
  5. Invest in products that have the most potential
  6. Reap rewards in ROI from smart investments

Implementation

We created this game using a combination of the Unity game engine and the AirConsole back-end framework to communicate between phones and the screen. The controller interface was created using Bootstrap 5. The artwork for the game was done by Amanda Federico and our music was created by Jakob Hasan. The game is submitted and under review by the AirConsole team!

Business Strategy

Since we used AirConsole technology to create the game, we have to put it onto their game store. AirConsole shares a portion of the profits they make with their developers. Therefore, we still must advertise the game ourselves to bring players in. As a brand new game studio, effective advertising will be essential to bringing this game to life. Our marketing strategy is still to be determined, but we anticipate that digital marketing, especially social media marketing, will be the most cost-effective way to reach our target market.
